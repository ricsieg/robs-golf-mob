iPhone (Safari only — must use Safari, not Chrome)

Open Safari and go to your Golf Mob URL
Tap the Share button (the box with an arrow pointing up) at the bottom of the screen
Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
Change the name to Golf Mob if you want
Tap "Add" in the top right
The green golf icon will appear on your home screen like a normal app


Android (Chrome)

Open Chrome and go to your Golf Mob URL
You should see a popup banner at the bottom saying "Add Golf Mob to home screen" — tap Install
If the banner doesn't appear, tap the three dots menu (top right)
Tap "Add to Home screen"
Tap "Install" or "Add"
The icon appears on your home screen
